About Our Prototype

This prototype shows how our app, LitterLogger, allows users to report litter, track clean-up efforts, and support environmental awareness around the River Kelvin.

It’s designed to be easy to use for both volunteers and the public, while collecting valuable data for FORK.

Key Features

Geo-tagged Reports

Pinpoint litter locations using real-time location tracking.

Image Upload

Capture and upload images of litter to assist clean-up volunteers.

Data Insights

Track trends in littering patterns and cleanup success rates.

Why This Prototype Matters

River Kelvin’s biodiversity is under threat due to increasing litter. Our prototype empowers communities to take meaningful action and contribute to a cleaner environment through simple digital reporting.
