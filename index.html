About Us

Why We're Building This

We are a team of Computing Science students from the University of Glasgow working on a project for the Object-Oriented and Interactive Programming module. Our goal is to empower environmental volunteers with technology.

Inspired by the challenges faced by Friends of the River Kelvin (FORK), we created this AI-powered app to assist in litter identification and data logging. This will help volunteers focus more on cleaning and less on paperwork, while also enabling data-driven insights into pollution trends.

Through this project, we aim to develop not just technical skills, but also meaningful solutions to real-world environmental problems.
