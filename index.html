Our Problem Statement

Some visitors of the River Kelvin are littering, maybe because they find it inconvenient to carry their waste to throw in bins, which pollutes the river and areas around it affecting the environment and the flora and fauna.

About Us

We are a team of Computing Science students from the Singapore Institute of Technology and the University of Glasgow, working on a project for our Overseas Immersion Programme in Glasgow. Our goal is to use modern design thinking to create effective solutions for social or environmental issues.

Through this project, we aim to develop not just our design and presentation skills, but also create a meaningful solution to a real-world environmental problem.
